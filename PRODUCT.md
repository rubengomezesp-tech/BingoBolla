# Product

## Register

product

## Users

BingoBolla is for adults 18+ in allowed US markets who want a social bingo and arcade-prize experience on mobile first, with desktop as a secondary surface. Users arrive to play quickly, understand their balances, progress through worlds, and enter bingo or mini-game sessions without needing to learn a complex app.

## Product Purpose

BingoBolla combines live bingo, mini-games, daily rewards, and sweepstakes-style prize progression in one Next.js and Supabase app. The worlds surface should make progression feel concrete: show what is unlocked, what is next, how many rewards are available, and where to play now.

## Brand Personality

Premium arcade, Miami energy, trustworthy rewards. The interface should feel bright, celebratory, and polished while keeping gambling-adjacent controls, balances, lock states, and responsible-play signals clear.

## Anti-references

Do not turn the product into a separate puzzle-tour app, a generic casino lobby, or a decorative landing page. Avoid fake glass panels everywhere, one-note purple gradients, unreadable neon text, oversized hero marketing, and interactions that hide locked or legal states.

## Design Principles

1. Progress is the main character: every world, card, node, and reward should explain where the player is and what unlocks next.
2. Trust beats spectacle: wallet values, KYC gates, locks, redirects, and no-purchase-required context must stay legible.
3. Mobile first, desktop complete: the world page should scan well on a phone and still use the wide desktop canvas intelligently.
4. One codebase, one truth: use the existing Next.js, Supabase, XP, and world tables rather than adding a parallel backend or frontend.
5. Delight is feedback: animation should confirm unlocks, active worlds, progress, and rewards, not decorate idle surfaces.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Support reduced motion, keyboard focus for navigable actions, clear disabled states for locked worlds, and text labels that do not rely on color alone.
